# Example: Dr. Zara Ali — Postdoctoral Researcher

> Fully filled-in Cowork governance templates for an academic researcher writing a monograph.

---

## About This Example

**Who:** Dr. Zara Ali, postdoctoral researcher in qualitative sociology (University of Edinburgh)
**Situation:** Writing a 90,000-word monograph on urban food governance while managing journal article submissions and a funding application
**Main challenge:** Citation integrity — a fabricated reference in academic writing is career-ending. This collaboration has one absolute rule and a documented failure episode that created it.
**Collaboration stage:** Month 8 (mature partnership, well-established rules)

## Key Patterns to Notice

**The citation prohibition is absolute.** F-001 happened in month 1 — Claude fabricated a Mouffe citation that sounded entirely plausible. The book did not exist. The prohibition that followed has zero exceptions: Claude writes `[CITATION NEEDED]` and stops. In eight months of collaboration, no second citation fabrication has occurred.

**Evidential language has levels.** The Truth Protocol defines four tiers: no evidence, single example, pattern across cases, robust findings. Claude must match the language to the tier. "Demonstrates" requires robust evidence. "Suggests" requires a pattern. The F-003 failure happened because "make this more forceful" was interpreted as an invitation to upgrade the evidential language beyond what the data supported.

**Near-misses are logged.** The Corrections Log in the Running Document and the near-miss table in FAILURE-RECOVERY.md both show that the collaboration treats near-misses as seriously as confirmed failures. The January 2025 near-miss — Claude began writing a real citation Zara hadn't provided — confirmed that the absolute prohibition catches real risk.

**Deadlines are tracked with calculated urgency.** The June 15 Chapter 4 deadline is 4 weeks away at the time of writing. The June 30 JUS article deadline is 6 weeks away, with the article not yet started. CANONICAL-NUMBERS.md shows this explicitly — Claude can see the urgency without being told.

## What This Example Demonstrates

A mature collaboration in a high-stakes context:
- One absolute rule with a documented origin (citation prohibition created after F-001)
- Evidential discipline protocols that prevent argument inflation
- Word count tracking that makes chapter progress and deadline risk visible
- Three documented failures with clear root causes and changes made
- Folder structure that separates primary research data (read-only) from working drafts
